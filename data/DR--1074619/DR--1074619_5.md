### Here come da judges …

![Figure](fileoutpart26.png)

You call these guys judges? Never before in the revered annals of the Annual (nee Bloomington) Comedy Quartet Contest has a more incompetent cadre of so called adjudicators been assembled. Ridicule, condescension, vituperation, humiliation, intimidation, were the bywords as this smug, haughty crew continually trampled on virtually every self-respecting ego in our parade of uniquely talented foursomes. (Egads; the shame!)
However, in an amazing empathetic turnabout CRANIAL CABBAGE (above from left to rt.): Roger Stanfield, Jim Emery, Bruce Watson, and Chuck McKown) put on a welcome display of their rare talents as accomplished comedic songsters (although not always in strict Barbershop style). Belated well-earned huzzahs for you boys!
