Although not a member of the Minneapolis Chapter, Rochester’s Gary

![Figure](fileoutpart16.png)

Rogness has been a much-loved and special friend of many past and present Commodores. Sadly, this magnificent bass, notorious for his Hold That Tiger role with the 1982 LOL District Champion quartet, Downstate Express, has been afflicted with advanced colon cancer and began chemotherapy on June 1. Apparently his medical team is optimistic. Nonetheless, we certainly must keep him in our thoughts and prayers.
This is my final Sunshine Hotline for the Minneapolis Commodores for as some of you know we’re moving out of state to be nearer our children. It’s been a privilege to serve the Commodore community is this capacity over the last several years and I’m going to miss you guys. I’ve learned a great deal about singing from the Barbershop fraternity and our great directors who are elite, as far as I’m concerned, not only as skilled musicians but as persons of exemplary character.
