# No lie … it’s Dan True! “Barbershopper of the Year”
On Sunday afternoon, June 4, an overflow crowd of 120 Commodores and friends gathered at Mancini’s Char House in St. Paul for the Minneapolis Chapter’s 2017 Family & Friends Appreciation Night.
Following a pleasant social hour complete with Mancini’s hors d’oeuvre buffet and after Party Chairman Mark Ortenburger’s call to order, the crowd was blitzed by a raucous rendition of In the Navy reprising the cast of the Village People from our recent annual show, still led by that shy introverted Gary Cooper, who this time, however, was accoutered in Naval officers’ dress whites.
When the audience finally caught their collective breath, Emcee Dan Cole introduced our special guest Dan & Karen True quartet, CHORD SMASH, with our very good friend and erstwhile assistant director, James Estes singing baritone. The foursome led us on an attenuated four–part journey with a stop at Broadway’s Hamilton for a costumed rendition of I’ll Be Back and finishing with The Devil Went Down to Georgia, featuring a violin, two guitars, and James on the cowbell (with great vigor).
Dan then called upon our resident-reverend, Dave Wall, to offer grace which he did, musically, with the attendees
