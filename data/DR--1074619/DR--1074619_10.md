# Jim Richards Memorial Chord Candy # 12

## Steal Away a spiritual arranged by B. Chilcott, rearranged by Paul Paddock

![Figure](fileoutpart50.png)

Dr. Jim Richards
"Watch out, this one's a high one! Inspired by the version done by the King's Singers, which I've sung with my ensemble in Asheville, North Carolina, it puts a contemporary perspective on the spiritual. It emphasizes constructive dissonance (not consonance) to put strength and sound out at those altitudes, just like a trumpet quartet would. Really crush the slide at the end. It's meant to sound the proverbial trumpet and if you know the spiritual, the focus is wishful thinking about the Promised Land during hardships. Do this one with your friends at the afterglow and you'll wow everyone around you! Enjoy!"

# En garde, Fargo
Forsooth, your gauntlet has been picked up, brandished on high, and the Commodore loins have been girded for the upcoming joust on the melodic fields of the Duchy of Rochester.
Our minions under the command of Paul, Duke of Wigley and his battle -hardened generals, Sir Gary of Ja-cobsonia, Sir David of Speidelton, and Sir Dave, the Younger, of Be-chardville, together, have each declared by his sacred oath that the wind-buffeted, flood-ravaged forces of the Principality of Fargo shall not now or ever prevail in the arduous trials of harmonious combat.
To ensure that the knights of the Dukedom of Wigley cannot falter, the Duke’s armorers, under the leadership of Sir Mark, of Ortenburg, have unveiled “The JAKE”, a weapon of considerable power, unconventional in nature, that engenders an inner force that when with them, makes its possessors virtually invincible.
Defeat and the loss of “The Jake”
